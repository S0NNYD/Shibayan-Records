ABOUT

Shibayan Records produces touhou music and some of their tracks are very popular. It gives off a very chill vibe and I seem to study fine with it playing in the background. There are a few playlists, especially the Toho Bossa Nova Collection playlists that I seem to constantly replay and never get tired of.

One very popular song that they have produced is a song called "Tiny Little Adiantum", which also produced another popular remix of the song which is called "Omae Wa Mou" and this specific song blew up on the internet during 2020 quite a bit, overshadowing its original.
